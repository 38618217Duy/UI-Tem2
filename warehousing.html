Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our warehousing services

What is your warehouse storage capacity?

expand_more
Our flagship Hai Phong Logistics Center offers 6,000m² of modern warehouse space. We also operate a warehouse at Song Than, Binh Duong. Both facilities feature climate-controlled areas, organized racking systems, and 24/7 security monitoring.

Do you have a Warehouse Management System (WMS)?

expand_more
Yes! We use a state-of-the-art WMS that provides real-time inventory tracking, automated reporting, barcode scanning, and integration capabilities with your systems. You can access inventory data 24/7 through our online portal.

What security measures do you have?

expand_more
Our warehouses feature 24/7 CCTV monitoring, controlled access systems, security guards, fire detection and suppression systems, and comprehensive insurance coverage. All staff undergo background checks and security training.

How is warehousing priced?

expand_more
Pricing is based on storage space used (per pallet or m²), handling fees (in/out), and value-added services (pick & pack, kitting, etc.). We offer flexible contracts from short-term to long-term. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

Where are your warehouse locations?

expand_more
We operate warehouses in strategic locations: Hai Phong (6,000m² Logistics Center near Hai Phong port) and Song Than, Binh Duong (serving southern Vietnam manufacturers). Both locations offer excellent access to major transportation routes.
