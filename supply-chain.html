Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about supply chain management

What is supply chain management?

expand_more
Supply chain management is the coordination of all activities involved in sourcing, procurement, production, and logistics. It includes managing the flow of goods, information, and finances from supplier to manufacturer to wholesaler to retailer to consumer. Effective SCM optimizes these processes to reduce costs, improve efficiency, and enhance customer satisfaction.

What are the benefits of supply chain optimization?

expand_more
Benefits include: 20-40% cost reduction, faster delivery times, improved inventory management, better visibility and control, reduced waste, enhanced customer satisfaction, and increased competitive advantage. Optimized supply chains are more resilient and adaptable to market changes.

How long does implementation take?

expand_more
Implementation timeline varies based on scope and complexity. A basic optimization project may take 3-6 months, while comprehensive supply chain transformation can take 12-18 months. We work in phases to deliver quick wins early while building toward long-term goals. Initial improvements often visible within first 3 months.

What ROI can I expect?

expand_more
Typical ROI ranges from 200-400% within first year through cost savings and efficiency gains. Most clients see payback period of 6-12 months. ROI comes from reduced transportation costs, lower inventory levels, improved warehouse efficiency, and better resource utilization. We provide detailed ROI projections during the analysis phase.

Which industries do you serve?

expand_more
We serve diverse industries including: electronics, automotive, manufacturing, retail, e-commerce, food & beverage, pharmaceuticals, fashion, and industrial equipment. Our solutions are customized to each industry's unique requirements, regulations, and challenges. We have particular expertise in supporting Japanese manufacturers operating in Vietnam.
