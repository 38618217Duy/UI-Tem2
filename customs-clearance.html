Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about customs clearance services

What are FTA benefits?

expand_more
Free Trade Agreements (FTA) can significantly reduce or eliminate import duties. Vietnam has FTAs with many countries including Japan (AJCEP), EU (EVFTA), and ASEAN members. Proper FTA application can save 5-20% on import costs. We help you navigate FTA requirements and obtain necessary certificates.

What documents are required for customs clearance?

expand_more
Typical documents include: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading/Airway Bill, Certificate of Origin (if claiming FTA), Import/Export License, and any specific permits for regulated goods. Our team will guide you through all documentation requirements based on your specific shipment.

How long does customs clearance take?

expand_more
With proper documentation, customs clearance typically takes 1-3 days. Fast-track clearance can be completed in 24 hours for urgent shipments. Time may vary based on cargo type, inspection requirements, and completeness of documentation. We work to minimize clearance time through pre-clearance preparation.

What are customs clearance fees?

expand_more
Fees include our service charge for customs brokerage, government duties and taxes (if applicable), and any inspection fees. Duties are calculated based on HS code, declared value, and origin. FTA can reduce or eliminate duties. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific shipment.

What's the difference between EPE and FDI support?

expand_more
EPE (Export Processing Enterprise) companies have duty exemptions for imported materials used in export production. FDI (Foreign Direct Investment) companies may have investment incentives but different customs procedures. We provide tailored support based on your company status, ensuring you maximize available benefits while maintaining compliance.
