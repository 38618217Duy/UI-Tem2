Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our land transportation services

What's the difference between FTL and LTL?

expand_more
FTL (Full Truckload) means you rent the entire truck for your exclusive use - ideal for large shipments. LTL (Less than Truckload) means your cargo shares space with other shippers - perfect for smaller loads. FTL offers faster delivery and more control, while LTL is more cost-effective for smaller volumes.

How does cross-border transport to Thailand work?

expand_more
We use the East-West Economic Corridor for Vietnam-Thailand transport. Transit time is 3-4 days from Vietnam to Bangkok. We handle all customs documentation and border crossing procedures. This service costs about half of air freight while being much faster than ocean freight.

Can I track my shipment in real-time?

expand_more
Yes! All our trucks are equipped with GPS tracking systems. You can monitor your shipment's location in real-time through our online portal or receive regular status updates via SMS/email. We provide 24/7 visibility throughout the journey.

Do you provide cargo insurance?

expand_more
Yes, we can arrange comprehensive cargo insurance to protect your shipment against loss or damage during transit. Insurance coverage is optional but recommended for valuable cargo. Contact us for insurance rates and coverage details.

Which areas do you cover in Vietnam?

expand_more
We have 17 locations covering all major cities and industrial zones in Vietnam including Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, Hai Phong, Da Nang, and key industrial parks in Binh Duong, Dong Nai, Hai Duong, and more. We can deliver to virtually any location in Vietnam.
